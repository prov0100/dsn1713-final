About Us

Who We Are

At Strawberry Blonde Bakery, we are dedicated to creating delicious and inclusive baked goods that cater to various dietary needs. Our menu features a wide array of sweet and savoury baked goods. With locations in Kanata and Westboro, we can guarantee you will find your new favourite treat at Strawberry Blonde Bakery.

Mission Statement

Strawberry Blonde’s mission is to create a place where people with alternative diets and food allergies can buy a treat without having to worry about cross contamination, while also not having to  compromise on taste or aesthetics.

Absolutely everything we do is vegan, gluten-free, nut free, and kosher!

Catering

No longer will any person go hungry at your next meeting or get together! No-one, regardless of food sensitivity or dietary choice, will have to be left out. And believe us, our platters are so good, that everyone will be happy you ordered from us!
